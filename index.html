How it works

Three simple pieces that cover your basics.

Allan's Fin Vault borrows the good parts of budgeting tools – without forcing you into subscriptions or cloud accounts.

01 · Capture

Log what you spend and save

Quickly add expenses and income with simple categories. No complex setup, no “pro” version – just see what is really going in and out.

02 · Organise

Group by goals that matter

Privacy first

Your data stays with you.

The short version: Allan's Fin Vault does not collect or sell your personal finance data. The app keeps everything on your device unless you choose to back it up to your own Google Drive.

  • No account with us: you do not create an Allan's Fin Vault account and we do not see your transactions.
  • No analytics or ads: we do not run in‑app advertising, tracking pixels, or analytics that identify you.
  • Google is only for backup: when you turn it on, Google is used purely so you can store a backup file in your own Drive.
  • You stay in control: you can disconnect Google backup at any time and delete any backup files from your Drive.
